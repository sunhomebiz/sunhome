FAQ

This page answers most questions. If it doesn’t answer yours, feel free to contact us.

Do you allow pets?
  • All of our Parks allow pets with the exception of Ambassador Downs. Contact the Park Managers to learn the details of their Park’s pet policy.
What is your mission statement?

Our mission is to provide well-managed and friendly mobile home, RV, and apartment living at affordable prices for seniors and families.

What is your “Move-In Process”?

Our Move-In Process is designed to be fast. First we run a background check. Reports usually come back within a few hours, but in rare cases might take a few days. If the results are approved, the Park Manager gives you a “move-in” packet of information. This information provides details about the Park, and your rights and responsibilities as a tenant. Once you sign that you have read and agreed to the terms, you will receive a lease agreement. The signed lease agreement will be submitted to the Corporate office to be checked for correctness and given final approval. The entire process can usually be completed within a day or two. Then you move in!

What are your rates?
  • Each Park is different, but all of our Parks are competitively priced in relation to similar parks in the area. Click on the Parks to see their Rates.
Do you give Reservation refunds?
  • Only if you cancel at least 60 days before your arrival date, or can provide a written medical report from a physician explaining why you could not come.
How do I book a Vacation Reservation?

Contact the Park Manager to book your reservation.
