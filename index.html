Sun Home Communities

AFFORDABLE AND FRIENDLY MOBILE HOME & RV PARKS IN MESA, APACHE JUNCTION, AND TUCSON ARIZONA

Sun Home Communities owns and operates seven affordable Arizona Mobile Home & RV Parks / Communities in the Phoenix East Valley and Tucson. Choose from five 55+ Mobile Home Parks, and two All-Age Mobile Home Communities. All of our communities have on-site managers and warm, friendly atmospheres.

We have mobile homes and spaces for both full-time tenants and winter visitors. In our communities you’ll find lots of friendly neighbors, and prices you can afford. If you’re a Winter Visitor, you’ll “Come for the Sun and Stay for the Fun!”
