About Us

Did you know? Some facts about us!

Buy a Mobile Home

Did you know you can live in your own Mobile Home for less than it costs to rent an apartment? It’s true! We have inexpensive, pre-owned Mobile Homes and Park Models for sale to suit any budget.

Move in Fast

Our Move-In Process is designed to be fast. The entire process can usually be completed within a day or two. Then you move in!

Pets Welcome

All of our Parks allow pets with the exception of Ambassador Downs. Contact the Park Managers to learn the details of their Park’s pet policy.

How much do your homes cost?

Home prices can start as low as $1,000, and range up to $25,000. Click on each Park to view homes for sale, or call the Park Managers to see what they have available.
